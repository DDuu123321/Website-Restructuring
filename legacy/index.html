HOW IT WORKS

One system,
five intelligent flows of energy.

Sun, battery, home, grid and car — Bluven's engineered controller decides where every kilowatt goes, second by second.

  1. Generate

    Tier-1 panels capture sunlight and feed DC into the hybrid inverter.

  2. Power your home

    Solar serves your home first — appliances, A/C, hot water — used instantly.

  3. Charge the battery

    Excess energy stores in the battery, ready for evenings when the sun's gone.

  4. Charge the car

    Smart routing sends spare sunshine to your EV — petrol bills, gone.

  5. Export to grid

    Anything left flows back to the grid as feed-in credits on your power bill.
