Our story

One van. One promise.

Founder Liam Walsh was an electrical engineer who'd spent ten years installing rooftop solar across Sydney's North Shore. He kept hearing the same complaint from customers: "the salesman was great — but I never saw them again."

So he started Bluven with a different idea: every job designed by an engineer, installed by an engineer, and supported by the same engineer for the life of the system. No call centres. No outsourced installers. No "head office overseas".

Today we're 38 people across NSW, VIC and QLD — but the model is the same. The person who quotes your job is on-site for the install. And the engineer who signs it off is one phone call away for the next 25 years.

What we believe

Three rules. Twelve years. No exceptions.

RULE 01

Engineer the whole system, not the panels.

A solar system isn't 16 panels — it's panels, an inverter, a battery, a switchboard, a meter and your daily energy habits. We design all of it as one connected stack. That's why our predicted yields match real numbers.

RULE 02

The person who quotes is the person who installs.

No bait-and-switch. No cheapest-tradie scramble on the morning of install. The engineer who walks your roof writes your design and signs off your commissioning report.

RULE 03

Local support, forever.

Every install includes 10+ years of workmanship warranty and direct access to the engineer who built it. Not a call centre. Not a chatbot maze. A real human, in your timezone, who knows your switchboard.
