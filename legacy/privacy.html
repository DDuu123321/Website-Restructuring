1. Introduction

Bluven Energy Pty Ltd (ABN 42 123 456 789) ("Bluven", "we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This policy describes how we collect, hold, use and disclose personal information in line with the Australian Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the 13 Australian Privacy Principles (APPs).

This policy applies to information we collect via bluven.com.au, the Bluven Live app, in person at our showrooms, by phone, email or any other channel.

2. What we collect

To design and install your energy system we typically need:

  • Identity & contact — name, address, email, phone, date of birth (only where required for finance applications).
  • Property data — site address, roof imagery, switchboard photos, NMI (National Metering Identifier).
  • Energy use data — interval consumption from your retailer (with consent), bill copies, appliance data.
  • Financial data — payment details, finance applications (handled by our partners under their own policies).
  • Technical data — IP address, device, browser, pages viewed, system performance telemetry from installed equipment.

3. Why we collect it

We only collect what's reasonably necessary to:

  • Design and quote your system, schedule install, and submit STC / federal / state rebate paperwork.
  • Operate the Bluven Live monitoring app and dispatch service technicians when systems fault.
  • Comply with Clean Energy Council, ARENA, AER and ATO requirements.
  • Communicate with you about your account, warranty and (only with consent) marketing.

4. How we use it

We use your data only for the purposes listed in section 3 or for closely related secondary purposes you'd reasonably expect — for example, sending warranty reminders or processing a service callout.

5. Who we share with

  • Equipment manufacturers (e.g. Tesla, SiGenergy, Sungrow) for warranty registration and remote diagnostics.
  • Your electricity retailer & distributor for grid connection approvals, NMI configuration and feed-in tariffs.
  • Government agencies for rebate processing (Clean Energy Regulator, state schemes).
  • Finance partners if you choose green finance — only with explicit consent.
  • Service providers bound by confidentiality (cloud hosting in Australia, AI assistants for chat triage).

We never sell your personal information.

6. Cookies & analytics

bluven.com.au uses essential cookies for session and security, plus opt-in analytics (Google Analytics 4 with anonymised IPs). You can manage preferences via the cookie banner or your browser settings.

Our AI chat assistant ("Sunny") may log conversation transcripts to improve service quality; chats are retained for 90 days and never sold or shared with third parties.

7. Security

Personal data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256) on Australian-hosted infrastructure (AWS Sydney). Access is role-based and audited. We notify the OAIC and affected individuals within 72 hours of a notifiable data breach as required by law.

8. Your rights

Under the APPs you have the right to:

  • Access your personal information that we hold.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time.
  • Lodge a privacy complaint with us, then with the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) if unresolved.

Most requests are actioned within 14 business days at no charge.
